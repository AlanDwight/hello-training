Bridging Cultures: The Journey of Hello Training Japanese Language Center in Myanmar

Hello Training Japanese Language Center is located on Kabar Aye Pagoda Road, Bahan Township, and is a convenient place to travel, where young people can actively participate in the business community, and where they can develop human resources that can improve society from an international perspective, including political science, business philosophy, and Japanese culture and foreign language teaching. HelloTraining Japanese Language Center is a language and career support institution recognized by the Ministry of Higher Education and established in 2022 with the basic philosophy of developing truly global human resources that will support the economies of Myanmar and Japan in Asia. Based on this founding philosophy, the educational goal is to develop global human resources who can actively participate in the international community, especially in Japan, with full self-confidence. We are currently not only through regular Japanese language courses, but also through extracurricular education. The human resources of the future are people who are grounded in “international” and “humanity” and who are able to actively create the future.
