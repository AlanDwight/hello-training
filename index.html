Who We Are

Hello Training Japanese Language Center is located on Kabar Aye Pagoda Road, Bahan Township, and is a convenient place to travel and is a place where young people can actively participate in the business community and encourage human resources that can improve society from an international perspective. It is a place where people can learn foreign languages, including political science, business philosophy, and Japanese culture.

Message from Our Headmaster

Hello Training Japanese Language Center was established in 2022 as a language and vocational support institution officially recognized by the Department of Higher Education. It was founded with the fundamental vision of developing genuine global human resources that will contribute to the economic cooperation between Myanmar and Japan within Asia. Based on this founding philosophy, our educational goal is to nurture globally competent individuals who can actively participate in the international community—especially in Japan—with full confidence in themselves. At present, we are engaged not only in teaching the Japanese language through formal courses but also in providing extracurricular educational activities. We believe that future human resources should be rooted in both “internationalism” and “human values,” and possess the ability to proactively create and shape the future.

Vision and Mission

Our Vision

We place special emphasis on enhancing human resources by improving their language proficiency and intellectual communication skills. Our programs aim to develop individuals who can effectively contribute to solving various issues in the international community, particularly in Japan. We also focus on bridging students to engineering education institutions that benefit society, thereby nurturing valuable human resources. By acquiring knowledge and skills that prioritize the development of Japan and the global community, each student can improve their competencies through structured courses. In addition, students gain exposure to diverse cultures and values through exchange programs between Japan and the international community, allowing them to experience rich cultural interactions.

Our Mission

Students are given opportunities to engage in daily conversations and discussions with native speakers, creating a supportive and enriching learning environment. There are also many opportunities for students to develop their ability to collaborate and work effectively with others. With over a decade of experience of the founder in Japanese scholarship programs and human resource development, the staff—many of whom have studied at Japanese universities—work together by drawing on their valuable experiences to enhance education and promote the development of human capital.

    Comprehensive Academic Program at Hello Training Japanese Language Center

    Hello Training Japanese Language School offers specialized courses to prepare and prepare students for the Japanese Language Proficiency Test (JLPT). These courses are carefully designed to provide students with the language skills and knowledge they need to excel in the JLPT exam. Focusing on all components of the exam, including vocabulary, grammar, reading comprehension, and listening comprehension, students receive targeted instruction and practice to improve their performance on the exam. Experienced instructors guide students through comprehensive study materials and provide valuable insights and strategies for success. With thorough preparation and personalized support, students at Hello Training Japanese Language School are ready to face the JLPT exam with confidence and achieve their goals in Japanese.

    Career Guidance and Counseling Services

    Hello Training Japanese School provides comprehensive career guidance and counseling services to help students achieve their professional goals. Through personalized guidance and tailored advice, students are supported in navigating the complexities of the job market and in developing strategies for career advancement.
