Thazin Nwe: Leading Educationist and Founder of Hello Training JLC

Date of birth and age: 25 – 3 – 1971, 53 years old.
Birthplace: Manaung Island, Rakhine State.
Specialties: International Development. Economic and political science and security-related development.
In 1994, she served as a civil servant in the Ministry of Home Affairs and retired from the Special Intelligence Service in 2000.
In 1998, she graduated from Yangon University with a degree in History.
From March 2005 to March 2006, she completed a one-year Japanese language diploma at Tokai University in Japan.
From April 2006 to March 2007, she worked as an International Cooperation Studies researcher at Takushoku University for one year. From March 2007 to March 2009, he completed a Master's Degree in International Cooperation Studies at Takushoku University and continued his education in Japan.
In 2013, she served as the Managing Director of Jan'sYangonTrading co.ltd. Subsequently, she worked on technology development and marketing strategy in Myanmar with Asahi Glass Asia Pacific through a JV agreement with a Japanese company from 2014 to 2019, and from 2015 to 2018, he worked as a consultant on port operations, transportation systems, and construction standards with Mitsubishi Research Institute, which supervised and advised Japanese government development projects.
In 2022, she founded HELLO TRAINING Japanese Languages ​​Centers and is still leading it today.
